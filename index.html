Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app > Settings > Restore Purchases. If you previously purchased Premium on another device or reinstalled the app, your purchase will be restored automatically.

I paid but Premium isn't showing. What do I do?

Try these steps: 1) Close and reopen the app. 2) Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. 3) Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase. If it still doesn't work, email us.

Is the purchase a subscription?

No. All our apps use a one-time lifetime purchase of $2.99. You pay once and keep all premium features forever. There are no recurring charges.

How do I turn off notifications?

Open the app > Settings > toggle off Daily Reminder. You can also go to your device Settings > Notifications > find the app > toggle off.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Deleting the app will delete all data.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find the purchase, and request a refund.

Do you support iPad?

Yes. All our apps work on both iPhone and iPad running iOS 17 or later.
